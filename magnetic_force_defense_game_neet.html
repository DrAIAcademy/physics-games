Magnetic Force Diagram + Formulas

  • Magnetic force on moving charge: F = qvB sinθ
  • For v perpendicular to B: F = qvB
  • Circular path radius: r = mv / |q|B
  • Acceleration: a = F / m = qvB / m
  • Force is perpendicular to velocity and magnetic field.
  • Negative charge bends opposite to positive charge.

Simple Explanation

A charged particle moving in a magnetic field experiences a force perpendicular to its velocity. This force bends the path into a curve. In this game, you control the magnetic field to deflect incoming particles away from the shield.

NEET Traps

  • Magnetic force is zero if charge is at rest.
  • Magnetic force is maximum when velocity is perpendicular to magnetic field.
  • Magnetic force does no work because it is perpendicular to velocity.
  • Negative charge deflects opposite to positive charge.
  • Radius decreases when magnetic field or charge magnitude increases.

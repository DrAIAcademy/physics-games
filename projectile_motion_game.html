NEET Traps

  • At highest point, vertical velocity becomes zero but horizontal velocity remains non-zero.
  • Acceleration is always downward due to gravity.
  • 45° gives maximum range only for same launch and landing level.
  • Horizontal velocity remains constant if air resistance is neglected.
  • Complementary angles give same range only for same level projection.

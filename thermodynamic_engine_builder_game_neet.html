Formula Focus

  • First law: ΔQ = ΔU + W
  • Isobaric work: W = PΔV
  • Isochoric work: W = 0
  • Isothermal ideal gas: ΔU = 0, so Q = W
  • Adiabatic process: Q = 0, so ΔU = −W
  • Ideal gas equation: PV = nRT
  • Work done is area under P-V graph.

Simple NEET Explanation

Thermodynamics connects heat, work and internal energy. When gas expands, it can push a piston and do work. The type of process decides how pressure, volume, temperature, heat and work change.

In this game, your engine runs successfully when the chosen thermodynamic process produces work close to the target mission value.

NEET Traps

  • Work is zero in isochoric process because volume does not change.
  • Internal energy of ideal gas depends only on temperature.
  • In isothermal process, ΔU = 0.
  • In adiabatic process, Q = 0.
  • Area under PV graph gives work done by gas.
  • Expansion gives positive work by gas.
